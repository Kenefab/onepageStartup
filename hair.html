Beautiful and Easy
The GeneySignatures Story

My name is Mmesoma Ukozor, I am the founder and CEO of GeneySignatures. I grew up watching hairstylists in my family run their businesses, and I noticed something… all of the hair products that our community was buying – we weren’t selling. That didn’t sit right with me. GeneySignatures began with the idea that if people are spending billions of dollars on beauty products, we should also have ownership in the businesses that sell those products. Furthermore, those products should be of the highest quality and delivered with world-class customer service.

Mmesomma Ukozor,
CEO GeneySignatures
